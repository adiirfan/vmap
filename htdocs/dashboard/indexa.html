Tutorial & FAQ Vmap Tutorial and Frequently Asked Question

General Explanation

Overview

In the Overview you can take a look summary of of the lab usage,here is the detail:

  • Computer Lab = This is the list of your Computer Lab
  • Lab Usage Percentage= Percentage of Lab Usage in your lab
  • Stations Available = Total PC that still available
  • Valid Till = Date of license key expire
  • Total PC Available = Total PC which is still not used for Vmap

Lab Design

Lab Design is separate by two part add/edit Lab and Manage Lab,here is the detail:

  1. Add / Edit Lab 
    •  To Upload the image to Map Editor (you can upload your lab map picture as the guide to design your lab in vmap)
    •  To load and edit your previous saved map
    •  To erase and clear your Map Editor
    •  To save your map
    •  PC icon for Mac
    •  PC icon For Windows PC
  2. Manage Lab
    •  You can select all lab,deselect all lab,and grouping every lab in group lab,and delete lab
    •  You can give general description to your lab using this 

Scheduling

In Lab Scheduling,you can manage your lab usage

  1. Scheduling
    •  Download your report into xlsx excel format
    •  Download your report into pdf format

Admin Management

In this menu you can manage the user who can accses the admin page of vmap,you can add by click add administrator on top,and click the magnify to see the user detail and you can delete it by click on the red trash icon

License Key

In this menu you can input your purchased license key to get more pc license for vmap
